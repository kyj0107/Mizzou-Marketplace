F.A.Q.

As a student or affiliate of Mizzou, using our platform lets you directly interact with your peers to get the resources you need, whether it be textbooks, furniture, or school supplies. When using larger scale services similar to ours, you may have a harder time finding products that are right for you. By using ours, you are allowed to buy textbooks from students that took the same classes you have to take.

  • Furniture
  • Textbooks
  • Clothing
  • School Supplies
  • Anything students need! Go ahead and click on 'Products' on the Navigation Bar to start looking!

No, there are no fees to using our website! As Mizzou Marketplace is a platform for students made by students, we understand money can be tight while paying for tuiton and other needs.

All payments should be done in person, in a public place. Mizzou Marketplace is not responsible for any scams that may result from payment online before you acquire your item. Please be careful and do not pay before you receieve what you buy. We are simply the middle-man for you to buy what you need!

With each listing, the buyer or seller will provide their email or phone number for you to reach out to. From there, you can decide between the two of you what price you agree on and where you would like to meet.
